# Code of Conduct

[简体中文](../../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [繁體中文](../zh-TW/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [日本語](../ja/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [한국어](../ko/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Our pledge

To foster an open, friendly, and respectful community, participants and maintainers pledge to make this project a harassment-free and discrimination-free collaboration space.

## Encouraged behavior

- Use welcoming, inclusive, and respectful language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Give constructive feedback focused on problems and improvements.
- Keep discussions aligned with project goals and user experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, harassment, threats, or repeated disruption.
- Discriminatory language based on identity, region, language, gender, age, ability, or similar attributes.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Spam, flame wars, off-topic disruption, or behavior that harms collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remind participants, edit or remove inappropriate content, close discussions, limit further interaction, or take other reasonable steps to protect the collaboration environment.

## Scope

This code applies to issues, Pull Requests, discussions, code reviews, and other public collaboration spaces related to this project.
